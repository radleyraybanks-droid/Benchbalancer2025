FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing accordingly.
What's the difference between Pro and Elite?
Pro gives you unlimited matches and full statistics for one sport. Elite unlocks all sports (Soccer, Basketball, Tag Football, AFL) under one subscription - perfect for coaches who manage multiple teams or sports.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your match history and player statistics are saved permanently. You can always view past data, but you'll be limited to 3 new competitive matches on the free tier.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! Every account starts with 3 free competitive matches. This lets you experience the full game-day features before deciding to upgrade.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
